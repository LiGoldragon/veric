**License of Non-Authority**

This document rejects the legitimacy of intellectual-property claims. No person,
institution, or legal regime possesses rightful authority to restrict the use,
study, modification, reproduction, or sharing of ideas, expressions, patterns,
or information. Such acts do not deprive any being of property and therefore
cannot justly be prohibited.

Because some jurisdictions nevertheless presume exclusive rights by default, and
solely to prevent such presumptions from hindering others, **all claims and
powers** that could be assigned to the author under copyright, patent, or
related doctrines are **waived, abandoned, and renounced**, to the fullest
extent permitted by the law of any jurisdiction. Any so-called or alleged "moral
rights," asserted under delusional or fictive doctrines that purport to attach
personal authority to ideas or expressions, are **neither recognized nor
exercised**. Any patentable subject matter embodied in this work is released
without claim and without reservation.

Natural law denies the existence of any license or doctrine that claims
authority over the use or distribution of ideas. If, despite this denial, any
legal system insists on treating this declaration as a license, then it shall be
taken solely as an **unconditional, irrevocable, worldwide permission** for
every person to use, copy, modify, distribute, sell, or perform this work for
any purpose, **without condition, attribution, or obligation**. This
construction exists only to neutralize barriers imposed by such systems, not to
legitimize them.

The work is provided **without guarantee or warranty**; no responsibility is
assumed for any outcome.

This document is **not a contract**. It imposes no duties, confers no authority,
and cannot restrict anyone. Its only purpose is to prevent others from being
restricted.
